Company Currency Total in Purchase V12
======================================
This module will show you the total amount of the purchase order in company currency in the tree view

Features
========

* Total amount in company currency in purchase order, RFQ, vendor bill and in creadit note

Tech
====
* [Python] - Models
* [XML] - Odoo views

Installation
============

- Install our custom addon
