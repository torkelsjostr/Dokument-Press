Purchase Product Consumption Info

This module display information icon in Purchase lines to know available quantities, Last Week Consumption and Last Month Consumption for each internal location

This module works on Enterprise and Community Edition.

Open Purchase Module, click on "Requests for Quotation" menu under "Orders" menu.


In the Purchase Lines click on Info Icon


Product Consumption information will appear which contain:

Location: Location Name.

Qty: Available Quantity in the location.

LWC: Last Week Consumption, all outgoing and internal pickings from the mentioned location for the last 7 days.

LMC: Last Month Consumption, all outgoing and internal pickings from the mentioned location for the last 30 days.
